Connections Results Fixer

This site allows you to copy your results from Connections and have two spaces automatically added to the end of each line, making the lines appears "stacked" when sharing to platforms that use Markdown, such as Reddit.

To use this tool, copy your results from Connections as you normally would, paste them into the box below, and click "Fix It!"

The fixed results will be automatically copied to your clipboard, ready to share.
