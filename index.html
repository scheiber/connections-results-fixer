ResultsFixer


About

This site allows you to copy your results from games such as Connections and Strands and have two spaces automatically added to the end of each line, making the lines appears "stacked" when sharing to platforms that use Markdown markup language, such as Reddit and Lemmy.

To use this tool, copy your results as you normally would, paste them into the box above, and click "Fix It!"

The fixed results will be automatically copied to your clipboard, ready to share.
